Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about i.lec and how we provide emotional support

What exactly is i.lec?

i.lec is a private emotional companion powered by AI. Think of us as a safe, judgment-free space where you can vent, unload thoughts, and reflect. We're not therapists or medical professionals—we're compassionate listeners available whenever you need to process your day or emotions.

How is i.lec different from therapy or counseling?

Therapy involves diagnosis, treatment plans, and clinical interventions for mental health conditions. i.lec provides non-clinical companionship—we listen, reflect, and provide emotional support without diagnosis or treatment. We're like a compassionate friend who's always available to listen, not a medical professional.

Is my privacy protected?

Absolutely. Your conversations are not reviewed by humans. We use end-to-end encryption and don't store personal conversations long-term. You remain in complete control of your data—you can delete conversation history at any time. Privacy is foundational to our design.

What happens if I'm in crisis or need professional help?

i.lec is not a crisis service. If you express thoughts of self-harm, harm to others, or are in immediate danger, we'll provide clear directions to appropriate emergency services and encourage you to seek professional help immediately. We maintain strict boundaries about redirecting to appropriate care.

Is i.lec available in Brunei and other SEA countries?

Yes, i.lec is designed with deep cultural sensitivity for Brunei and Southeast Asian contexts. We understand local values around privacy, family, and emotional expression. Our communication style is discreet, respectful, and appropriate for these cultural contexts.
