Common Stress Indicators

Sleep Patterns

Difficulty falling asleep, waking up frequently, or sleeping too much can indicate elevated stress levels and disrupted circadian rhythms.

Physical Symptoms

Headaches, muscle tension, fatigue, or stomach issues may be physical manifestations of chronic stress affecting your body.

Cognitive Changes

Racing thoughts, memory problems, brain fog, or difficulty concentrating can be stress-related cognitive impacts.

Social Withdrawal

Isolating yourself from friends and family or avoiding social situations may signal stress overload and emotional exhaustion.
