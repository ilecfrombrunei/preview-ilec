What is i.lec?

Your Intelligent Learning Empathetic Caregiver

i.lec is an AI-driven digital wellness platform designed to provide accessible, 24/7 mental health support. We combine cutting-edge technology with human-centered design to create a compassionate companion that grows with you.

Born from the need to address the silent mental health crisis in Brunei and beyond, i.lec represents a new era of preventive mental healthcare. We're not just another app—we're your pocket Zen master for stress management and emotional well-being.

Our platform learns from your interactions, adapts to your unique needs, and provides evidence-based support exactly when you need it most—especially during those challenging evening hours when traditional support is unavailable.

The Story Behind i.lec

Born from Compassion

Created by people who care about community well-being

A Vision Born from Community Need

The idea for i.lec emerged in 2024 as we witnessed our community in Brunei grappling with growing mental health challenges. We saw friends, family members, and colleagues struggling silently with stress, anxiety, and emotional fatigue—especially during evenings and weekends when professional help was hardest to find.

The COVID-19 pandemic amplified what was already a silent crisis, with anxiety levels rising by 28.4% and people of all ages facing unprecedented challenges. We knew we couldn't stand by and watch—we had to create something that would be there for people when they needed it most.

What started as a conversation among concerned friends evolved into a mission: to make mental wellness support accessible, affordable, and available 24/7 for everyone who needs it.

The Accessibility Gap

Traditional mental health support systems face significant challenges:

  • Limited Hours: Most support is only available during business hours
  • High Costs: Professional therapy can be prohibitively expensive
  • Geographic Barriers: Limited availability in rural areas
  • Long Wait Times: Weeks or months for appointments

i.lec exists to bridge these gaps, providing immediate, affordable, and accessible support to anyone with an internet connection.
